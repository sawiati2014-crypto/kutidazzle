About the Giveaway

This holiday season, we're spreading love and support to a deserving bride in our community. We understand that planning a wedding can be financially challenging, and we want to help make your special day even more beautiful.

Only ONE bride will be selected for this special giveaway based on genuine financial need. Our team at Kuti Dazzles is committed to supporting our community and helping brides feel confident and radiant on their wedding day.

This is our heartfelt way of giving back and making a difference in someone's life during this festive season.

Eligibility & Selection

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be a bride genuinely in financial need
  • Wedding must fall between 1st – 31st January 2026
  • Must be 18+ years old
  • Open to Tamale residents only
  • A short explanation or proof of need is required
  • All shared information is confidential
  • Booking period: 1st December – 31st December 2025
  • Booking is only confirmed after receiving written confirmation from Kuti Dazzles

Selection Process

  • Only one bride will be chosen for this special giveaway
  • Selection is based solely on demonstrated financial need
  • The final decision is made by the Kuti Dazzles Beauty Parlor team
  • All applications are treated with respect and confidentiality
  • The selected bride will be notified directly via phone or WhatsApp

What's Included in the Offer

What's Included

  • Professional bridal makeup on the wedding day
  • A brief virtual or in-person consultation before the wedding
  • Use of premium, skin-friendly makeup products
  • Long-lasting makeup application techniques

What's Not Included

  • Hair styling and accessories
  • Makeup for bridesmaids or family members
  • Transportation to and from the venue
  • Any additional beauty services

Note: Additional services can be booked at our regular rates

Terms & Conditions

Photos & Consent

With the bride's permission, we may take before-and-after photos and short video clips. These will be used strictly for promotional purposes for Kuti Dazzles Beauty Parlor and will always respect the bride's privacy and dignity.

Cancellation Terms

If the bride cancels or changes the wedding date and we are unavailable for the new date, the offer becomes void. Kuti Dazzles may withdraw or reschedule in case of emergencies, with as much notice as possible provided.

Non-Transferability

This offer cannot be transferred, sold, or exchanged for cash. It applies only to the selected bride and cannot be used by anyone else.
